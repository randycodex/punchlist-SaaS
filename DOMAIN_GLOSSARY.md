# Domain Glossary (NYC)

## Property identifiers
- BBL: Borough-Block-Lot identifier.
- Tax lot: parcel identifier; zoning lot may differ from tax lot(s).
- Zoning lot: zoning-defined lot; may be assembled/merged across tax lots.

## Zoning programs / overlays
- MIH: Mandatory Inclusionary Housing.
- QH: Quality Housing.
- Transit Zone: zoning-defined transit zone affecting parking.
- E-Designation: environmental requirements (hazmat, attenuation, ventilation) tied to a property.

## District terms
- Zoning district: e.g. `R8A`.
- Commercial overlay: e.g. `C2-4`.
- Special district: e.g. “Special Jerome Corridor District”.

## Workflow statuses (zoning items)
- `auto_filled`: sourced from a lookup or known input.
- `calculated`: computed from explicit inputs (still reviewable).
- `guidance`: interpretation notes; not a pass/fail.
- `manual_review_required`: must be reviewed by a human before relying on it.
